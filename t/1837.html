MPOGD (Multiplayer online games directory) has a game of the month poll athttp://www.mpogd.com/.

Click on the small VOTE! link to vote.

The Multiplayer Online Games Directory's (MPOGD) Game of the Month is chosen by players as the premier game for that month. All playable games listed are capable of winning this award although a game can win the award only once a year.

"This is a good way to maybe attract new players to Freelancer and also this community".

Check out the in the VOTE for Freelancer! post by w0dk4 in the Freelancer Discussion Forum.

Have a look at this site: Click here

Every month, you can vote your favourite game on a daily base (you can vote once every day!).

If your game wins, it will stay on top of the page for the next month.

This is a good way to maybe attract new players to Freelancer and also this community.

You can vote here: Click here
