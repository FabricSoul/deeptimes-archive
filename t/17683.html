原作者：brucewoo　发表：2008-7-27　来源：bbs.deeptimes.net（已关站）· Wayback 存档

#1

在公司加班物料，捣鼓了一下爆裂服规则的英文稿。

翻译时对规则作了同义合并或一点节略。

由于水平有限，以及对规则未能了解透彻，因此先在这里贴上来，征求一下大家的意见：

=========================

以下为爆裂服规则英文稿：

=========================

★        General

1.        Any modifications to ER MOD/game files or using hook programs will be regarded as cheating.

2.        Utilizing any bugs was forbidden.

3.        PVP was forbidden in the capitals (New York, New Berlin, New London, New Tokyo)

4.        You can not attack the player who is docking or launching.

5.        Make sure you have less than 20 pieces of dismounted equipment in you cargo, or they will be lost.

★        Protections for newbies

1.        Players under the level of 50 grades will be regarded as newbies and protected.

2.        You can NOT engage any newbies. (Except for PVP agreed by both sides)

3.        Newbies can NOT engage with other players. (Except for PVP agreed by both sides)

★        RPG (Role playing) rules

1.        Player will be named automatically as member of a certain party as he gets high enough reputation. It is the RPG character of player. You can type “/list” in public chat channel to view the naming of all players online.

2.        Xeno and Corsair were regarded as “Illegal forces”. The other parties are “Legal forces”.

3.        Players respectively belong to Illegal forces and Legal forces can engage with each other. (except for the circumstance of “Protections for newbie” )

4.        Players of Legal forces can not engage with each other(Except for PVP agreed by both sides)

5.        Players of Illegal forces have the right of robbery on any other players above 50 grades.

★        Clans

1.        Clan is a special RPG entity, manually defined by server administrator. There are 2 classes of Clans.

2.        Clan is acquiescently legal force.

3.        Class-1 Clan owns one space station as headquarter. The galaxy of the headquarter will be regarded as the domain of the Clan. The Clan has right to levy taxes on other players pass by, and attack those refused to pay taxes. (except for the circumstance of “Protections for newbie” )

4.        Galaxies within two jumps from the domain are also the orbit of the Clan. The Clan has the right to use the resources with priority.

5.        Class-2 Clans can not own any domain.

6.        Players of Clans can not assemble to enter the domain of other Clans without permission. Otherwise it will be regarded as invasion and those players will be engaged.

★        Excessive engage

1.        You can not shot down the same player more than 6 times within 24 hours.

2.        You can not badger with the attacker after being shot down for 6 times.

3.        PVP agreed by both sides is unlimited.
