自从进入2018年，Egosoft就没有发布过X4相关的视频，除了30周年视频的最后的一小段。作为一款预计2018年内发售的游戏，到现在为止也不见有宣传。

作为一家小型德国游戏工作室，四大游戏展之一的德国科隆游戏展可以说是最近最好的平台了，但名单里也不见踪影。

带着这种疑问，我在X4官方Reddit上发问，得到如下回复：

https://www.reddit.com/r/X4Found ... s_in_gamescom_2018/

问1. X4会否出现在科隆展？

答1：会，我们的游戏将会向预约的记者群体进行展示，但现场没有摊位。如果你是记者，可联系press@egosoft.com。（没有摊位可以理解，毕竟是小工作室并且X4是没有发行商的）

问2. X4是否还按预定在2018年发行？

答2：是

X4 - Teladi Osprey

07/24/2018

https://imgur.com/gallery/ryn8kS6

A story from the X4Foundations universe. Showing and explaining the Teladi Osprey. In the next few days we will add more pictures that show details of this ship.

It has one S dock here with a Teladi Kestrel.

来自X4宇宙的故事。展现了Teladi族M级船Osprey。往后几日会放出更多关于这飞船的图片。

如图所见，这飞船有一个S级飞船的停泊位，上面停着架Teladi族S级船Kestrel

The Osprey is an M size frigate in this case equipped with two turrets and one front mounted missile launcher.

Osprey是一艘M级护卫舰，如图所示着艘装有两座炮塔和一个位于正面的导弹发射器

The dock can store away ships to the inside of a ship.

停泊处可以把小型船收进船舱内

The Osprey of course can only fit very few ships inside, but larger ships can host quite some numbers.

Osprey只能停进少量的船，相对的更大型的船能收纳进更多小船

I climbed into the Kestrel cockpit. There is a pilot here, so the ship can immediately respond to commands OR I simply click on the chair and take control myself.

我的角色进入Kestrel的驾驶舱。一位飞行员已经就位可执行各种命令，或者我的角色直接坐下自己操作

But now, lets go to the Cockpit of the Osprey by using the transporter room down this ramp.

但是，现在让我们先沿着斜坡进入Osprey驾驶舱

This menus shows that there is another Kestrel stored away. I click on the Osprey cockpit... (more coming tomorrow)

菜单显示有另一架Kestrel已停在内部。我选择进入Osprey的驾驶舱（明天发布更多图片）

X4 Osprey Docking

7/27/2018

https://imgur.com/gallery/CqJMApQ

#X4Foundations - This is the The shipyard we are docking at from a distance. L and XL ships would dock from the side, while an M ship like the Osprey can land on a platform.

前面是我们将要停靠的船坞。L级和XL级的大船要停在旁边（应该是指那些手臂），像Osprey这样的M级船则可停在平台上

Next the Telaid Osprey itself will dock. We are approaching a Teladi Shipyard. Another "M" size ship is still docking, so we have to wait a moment.

现在Teladi族M级船Osprey会自动停泊。这是Teladi族的船坞。另一艘M级船已经停靠了，我们要等一下

Approaching the dock. You can see one M dock and several S docks around it. These are just size categories. Very different types of ships can share the same size of dock.

接近船坞中，你可以看到出面前有一个M级停泊点和几个S级停泊点。当然，这只是体量上的区分，不同类型的船能共享同样大小的停泊点？（我也不知道这一句在说什么）

UI showing me where to land. Here in external view.

游戏UI会提示我在哪里停靠。这是外部视角

Landing the Teladi Osprey on one of this shipyards M docks.

停靠中

Ships can be extended with different docking computer extensions, to make this easier.

飞船可以加装不同的停靠辅助电脑配件，让一切更容易

We are docked - The menu allows quick actions on the station without getting out.

我们停好了。我们不用离开船就可以利用菜单与船坞进行互动和下达指令

I get up from my chair to leave my ship nevertheless.

我现在离开座位

An NPC pilot takes over the seat in case I want to send the ship away.

一个NPC飞行员就会出现并且坐下，等待我对船只下达指令

Nice view from up here in the Osprey cockpit.

从Osprey驾驶舱望去的好风景

Next I will get out and show the ship from down there!

下一篇我会走出驾驶舱，从船坞视角展示这艘船
