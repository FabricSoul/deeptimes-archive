X3 Plugin Manager by Cycrow (脚本安装管理器)

This is an unofficial utility that can be used to install scripts and mods. Note that not all scripts are compatible with this utility but virtually all well developed scripts are due to it's ease of use. Only script packages that end in .spk will work with this utility. As it is unofficial, scripters are not required to use this utility. Check in the thread of the script you are interested in to see if it supports this program.

Ship Manager By Cycrow (飞船安装管理器)

This is an unoffical utility that is used to create and install ship mods. Any xsp files require this program to install them and allows ships to be added onto current mods

MOD

Mod 与 Scripts 是不同的，Mod 一般同时只能用一个，他们往往是两个文件*.cat+*.dat，需要放在X3的mod目录下面，然后在启动画面选择。大的mod会自带一些Scripts，主要是为了把扩展的船加入的游戏中。你可以把mod看成是声明了一些类，然后配套的scripts把它们实例化。

1, XTENDED MOD 0.6b

http://forum2.egosoft.com/viewtopic.php?t=117990

http://www.thexuniverse.com/index.php/board,18.0.html

这个mod致力于对原有游戏进行风格一致的扩展，加进了一个M0及相关任务，M7和M8，加强了三个敌人的实力（Pirate, Xenon, Khaak），加入一个新族，另外还有很多其他船，但都符合游戏原有风格。加强了游戏中战斗的视觉效果。加进了一些新的任务。这个mod的质量比较高。但是可惜他们不与论坛中其他mod组共享成果，所以在其他mod中没有任何这个mod的船。这个mod不用从头重新开始游戏。

这个mod会生成一些比较强的pirate船队，好像已经有人抱怨敌人太强了。暂时这个mod与Race response fleet有一点小冲突，是关于M0 Mission的。以后应该可以修正。其他features&bugs请看他们的论坛。下一个Patch，3周内发布。

ftp://tomol.widge.org:52100/PatchForXtm0.6b2.zip

我做的patch，除去和cycrow homebase cmd的冲突。现在和 public 的 Xai Corp. Remote Transporter Device的有冲突，但是和bonus pack里面的Xai Corp. Remote Transporter Device不冲突。

2, DDRS (maintained by DeadlyDa and Red Spot) 2.3.8

http://forum2.egosoft.com/viewtopic.php?t=126698

这个mod在TLF论坛出现过，就是所谓的星战mod，其实新加的船大多跟星战没有关系。这个mod的最大特点是，可以自己建造船，当然你必须向工厂喂各种资源，另外花的时间也比较长。这个mod的质量也相当不错。

这个mod几乎每个星期都会升级，每次升级都会加进新船。在Red Spot完成他新版的scripts以后会更新到 version 3，到时候有可能会和version 2的存档不兼容。

这个mod有很多fans，大多是喜欢里面某个电影里的船。另外有个re-balance的DDRS就快要出了。

我在尝试合并DDRS和Xtended，我已经完成了0.6a和2.3.8的合并。但是需要很多测试，有可能会在DDRS出3的时候，再尝试发布在TLF。

3, Xperiment Fusion Project 0.2 beta

http://forum2.egosoft.com/viewto ... 18618cf82b4d85642c9

这个mod 添加了 140+ 的新船（几乎包括了所有官方论坛中的船，除了Xtended Mod），包括了几乎官方论坛中所有的scripts，添加了100+的新sector。可以说是个大杂烩。但是由于加了新sector所以和原有游戏不兼容，需要从头开始玩起。因为是大杂烩，所以我担心它的质量。我没有尝试过这个mod。

4, Firelance，无发布日期

这个mod对游戏的改动非常大，经济结构会完全不同，连seta都准备去掉，几乎是完全改变了游戏方式。关于这个mod的消息很少，但是很多人都对它非常期待。

5, Community Extended Mod Pack {EMP} v1.4

http://forum2.egosoft.com/viewtopic.php?t=119914

http://ircwhore.co.uk/x3/scripts/emp.spk

这个不是严格意义上的mod，而且它一般被安装成false patch，就是在X3根目录下的0X.cat/0X.dat。事实上07.cat/07.dat就是X3官方的false patch，1.4刚添进去的。False patch的好处显而易见，他可以和其他mod共存。DDRS和Xtended Mod已经包括了EMP 1.4。

EMP是为了让scripts writer可以使用更多的自定义wares，并且保证没有冲突。

B, AL Plugins

AL Plugin means artificial life plugin. 事实上这是一些运行在游戏后台的程序，player无法直接感觉到他们，也不能通过命令来影响他们。典型的 AL Plugins 做的事情就是加强 Xenon，Khaak和Pirates的侵略性。

1, X3 Race Response Fleets, v1.5

这个 scripts 主要是加强5个Race（Argon, Paranid, Boron, Teledi, Split）对外族侵略的反应。每个族都会有一些M1 M2 M6被选进Response Fleets，一旦自己宗族受到攻击，这些船就会jump到那里去提供保护。

http://forum2.egosoft.com/viewtopic.php?t=105272

2, X3 Player Response Fleets, v1.0

这个顾名思义，是在player的财产受到威胁的时候，你设定的一些船就会自己跑去保护。

http://forum2.egosoft.com/viewtopic.php?t=126081

3, Race Invasions, v9.1

刚才两个是保护，这个则是进攻。有的族有时会去进攻其他族的领地，并且有些族还会向你请求帮助，这可是加reputation的好机会，当然会影响你对另一族的rep（还没有尝试过，不敢肯定）。会互相开战的只有关系不怎么样的族之间，比如Argon-Paranid, Boron-Split.

http://forum2.egosoft.com/viewtopic.php?t=105154

4, RS.NPC.Plugins

由4个组成，不过可以分开安装。

a, RS.Hunt，会有人不断的追杀你。

b, RS.Mass Driver Suppliers，有一些船专门负责给别人供应Mass Driver以及子弹，因为电脑使用Mass Driver的太少了。

c, RS.ZzzZRbbnNnzZzz，会有一些 Khaak Cluster 不断的对随机的sector进行进攻。

ftp://tomol.widge.org:52100/RS.ZzzZR.spk

d, RS.11010011101，会有一个Xenon的舰队对随机的sector进行进攻。

我只装了第3个。第4个也不错，不过攻击性很强。记住一定要安装Race Response Fleets，否则电脑的sector很快被清空了。

http://forum2.egosoft.com/viewtopic.php?t=127464

5, Sector Takeover v 2.2

这个准许player完全占领一个sector，使他成为你的，而不是Argon或者Boron的。当然你也可以占领unknown sector.

http://forum2.egosoft.com/viewtopic.php?t=143524

6, Yaki Armada, v1.05

使Yaki更加活跃的scripts，通过这个plugin，你甚至可以和Yaki通信，还可以加入他们一起成为海盗。

http://forum2.egosoft.com/viewtopic.php?t=128310

IntroductionThis script adds various items on the BBS including ways to buy larger ships without the notority requirement, buying various upgrades and hacking reputation so races will like you again. Well you can pay for someone to upload the coordinates of all stations win the sector which also includes the hidden pirate bases. You can find the BBS entries at Trading stations or Pirate Bases.

Once you accept, the money will be removed from your account and the stations will now appear on your sector map. 3. Gate PositionsLike the station positions, you can also pay to reveal all the gates in the sector if your having trouble finding them. You just pay the fee and the gates will then appear on the sector map.

4. Buying Capital ShipsHaving trouble getting notority for the race you wish to buy the larger ships from ? Well luckly the pirates have some for sale, you can find the pirates selling of these ships. However be warned, they will come abit damaged and can be expesive. On the BBS there will be 3 options, 1 for the M6, one for the M2 and one for an M1, the ships will be random each time. They are mainly available at pirate basses, but you can occasionally find them in other stations too.

Once you have paid them, they will give you the coordinates of where you can find the ship.

When you fly to the coordinates, you will find a group of yaki/pirate ships guarding the ship, just take them out and the ship is yours. 5. Goner DealerYou can sometimes find goner dealers on the BBS, these will sell you various upgrades for your ships, like the Jumpdrive or the Transporter device, they do however cost more than they would at the goner temple and only appear when you dont have them already installed on your ship. Once you accept your money will be taken and the device will be installed imediatly.

6. Insurance DealerYou can also find goners that will sell you salvage insurance in bulk from the BBS, like the dealers, they will also cost you more than usual but you can get them anywhere in the universe not just limited to the Goner Temple.

7. LotteryWant some easy money, or perhaps a free ship ? Well you could try your luck on the lottery and maybe you'll be a winner. There are various prizes available including ships and money. All you have to do is pick a ticket and hope you win.

8. Reputation Hack Need any races hate you ? need to get a quick boost in notority, well the pirates have what you need. You can only access the reputation hack when you have really bad notority and will only ever bring you up to neutral terms.
